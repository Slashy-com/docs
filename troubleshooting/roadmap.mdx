Slashy is built in close collaboration with users. Many current features started as user requests.

## Actively Being Built

- Mobile app improvements (performance, stability, new features)
- Calendar enhancements (timezone handling, availability)
- Agent capabilities (more tools, better accuracy, faster response)
- WhatsApp and Telegram agent channels
- Performance optimizations for large inboxes

## Submit a Feature Request

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email">
    Send your request to [founders@slashy.com](mailto:founders@slashy.com). Describe what you want, why it matters to your workflow, and how you imagine it working. The founders read every request.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Agent Sidebar">
    Open the sidebar (`Cmd+Shift+L`) and describe what you wish Slashy could do. The agent logs requests to the team.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>Requests with specific use cases get prioritized faster than vague suggestions. Tell us what you're trying to accomplish, not just what button you want.</Tip>

For updates on a submitted request, email [founders@slashy.com](mailto:founders@slashy.com) with your original request for context.
