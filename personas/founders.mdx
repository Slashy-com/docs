Founders live in email but can't afford to live in email. Slashy keeps your inbox under control so you can focus on building.

## Your Setup Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Importance Labels">
    Go to **Settings > Labels** and create VIP labels for board members, investors, and co-founders. Emails from these contacts jump to the top of your inbox automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Calendar">
    Link Google Calendar in **Settings > Calendars**. Slashy uses your schedule for meeting prep briefings and availability sharing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Train AI Drafts">
    Reply to 5-10 emails using `Cmd+Enter` to generate drafts, then edit before sending. Within a few days, drafts match your tone -- direct for team, polished for investors.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up iMessage Alerts">
    Connect your phone in **Settings > iMessage**. VIP alerts and dropped ball summaries arrive as texts so nothing slips through when you are away from your laptop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Key Snippets">
    Press `n s` to create snippets for intros, meeting requests, and investor updates. Insert with `Cmd+Shift+S` to save minutes per email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Automations For You

Open the AI agent sidebar (`Cmd+Shift+L`) and set up these automations:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Daily Dropped Ball Scanner">
    A morning iMessage listing every email you haven't replied to that likely needs a response. Catches the thread that slipped to page two while you were firefighting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Meeting Prep Briefing">
    15 minutes before each external meeting, Slashy sends a briefing with the attendee's recent emails, last meeting notes, and any open threads. Walk into every call prepared.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Receipt Forwarding">
    Automatically forwards receipts and invoices to your Expensify or Ramp email address. No more end-of-month receipt hunts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Investor Email Tracking">
    Enable open tracking (`p o` on any sent email) to see when investors open your fundraise updates. Follow up when they've read it but haven't responded.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pro Tips

- **Inbox zero in 10 minutes:** Use `j`/`k` to navigate, `e` to archive, `Cmd+Enter` to draft a reply, `Cmd+Shift+Enter` to send. Process 50+ emails without touching your mouse.
- **Board meeting prep:** Ask the agent *"Summarize all board member emails from the last 30 days"* before your next board meeting.
- **VIP alerts only:** Set iMessage alerts to VIP-only mode so you are not buzzed for every newsletter -- just the emails that actually matter.
- **Delegate with context:** Tell the agent *"Forward this thread to [ea@company.com] with a summary and ask them to schedule a follow-up."* Your EA gets the full picture without you retyping anything.

## Sample Agent Commands

Try these in the agent sidebar (`Cmd+Shift+L`):

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Inbox Triage">
    - *"What are my dropped balls right now?"*
    - *"Summarize my unread emails and tell me which need a reply today"*
    - *"Which investor emails am I behind on?"*
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Investor Relations">
    - *"Summarize all board member emails from the last 30 days"*
    - *"Draft a monthly investor update with key metrics placeholder"*
    - *"When did I last email [investor name]?"*
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Team & Delegation">
    - *"Forward this thread to [ea@company.com] with a summary and next steps"*
    - *"Draft a response to [team member] approving this and adding a deadline"*
    - *"What commitments have I made in email this week?"*
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
