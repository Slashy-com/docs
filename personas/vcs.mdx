VC work runs on relationships and speed. Slashy keeps you responsive to founders, organized on deal flow, and prepped for every pitch meeting.

## Your Setup Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set Up Founder Labels">
    Create importance labels for portfolio founders, active deal flow, and LPs. Founder emails surface at the top -- everything else waits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Train AI on Your Style">
    Reply to 5-10 founder emails using `Cmd+Enter` drafts and edit each one. The AI learns your voice fast so you can respond quickly without sounding generic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Calendar">
    Link Google Calendar in **Settings > Calendars**. Slashy uses it for meeting prep briefings and sharing availability with founders via `Shift+Cmd+A`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Deal Flow Snippets">
    Press `n s` to create snippets for pass emails, next-steps requests, intro forwards, and term sheet follow-ups. Insert with `Cmd+Shift+S`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable iMessage Summaries">
    Connect your phone in **Settings > iMessage** for weekly portfolio summaries and alerts when key founders email you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Automations For You

Open the AI agent sidebar (`Cmd+Shift+L`) and set up these automations:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Pitch Meeting Prep">
    Before every meeting tagged as a pitch, Slashy sends a briefing with the founder's recent emails, prior meeting notes, and any open threads. Walk into every pitch with full context.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Weekly Portfolio Summary">
    Every Monday morning, get an iMessage digest summarizing emails from all portfolio founders over the past week. Spot who needs attention without reading every thread.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Fast Reply Drafts">
    Slashy auto-drafts replies to founder emails within minutes. Review and send with one click. Founders notice when you respond in under an hour.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Deal Flow Tracker">
    Daily scan for inbound pitch emails you haven't responded to. A morning summary ensures no promising founder gets left on read.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pro Tips

- **Speed wins deals:** Use `Cmd+Enter` to draft, quick-edit, then `Cmd+Shift+Enter` to send. Founders remember the investor who responded in 20 minutes, not 2 days.
- **Portfolio pulse check:** Ask the agent *"Which portfolio founders haven't emailed me in 30 days?"* to spot who might need a check-in.
- **Warm intros in seconds:** When forwarding an intro, ask the agent *"Forward this to [founder] with context about why they should meet [person]."* The AI pulls relevant details from your email history.
- **Pass gracefully:** Create a snippet for pass emails with a warm, specific reason. Insert with `Cmd+Shift+S`, add one personal line, send. Respectful and fast.
