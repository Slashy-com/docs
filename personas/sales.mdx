Every unanswered email is a lost deal. Slashy makes sure no prospect falls through the cracks and every call starts with context.

## Your Setup Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Email Tracking">
    Press `p o` on any sent email to enable open tracking. You will see exactly when a prospect opens your message -- and how many times.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Outreach Snippets">
    Press `n s` to create snippets for cold outreach, follow-ups, pricing responses, and meeting confirmations. Insert with `Cmd+Shift+S` and personalize the last 20%.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Booking Links">
    Go to **Settings > Calendars > Booking Links** to create shareable scheduling pages. Drop them into emails with a snippet or ask the AI to insert availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Train AI on Your Voice">
    Send 5-10 replies using `Cmd+Enter` drafts, editing each to match your sales tone. The AI learns fast -- within days it handles routine replies on its own.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Label Your Pipeline">
    Create importance labels for prospects, active deals, and customers. Slashy auto-sorts incoming emails so hot leads always surface first.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Automations For You

Open the AI agent sidebar (`Cmd+Shift+L`) and set up these automations:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Follow-Up Nudge">
    If a prospect hasn't replied in 3 days, Slashy drafts a follow-up and queues it for your review. You approve with one click instead of manually tracking who ghosted you.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Meeting Prep Briefing">
    15 minutes before a prospect call, get an iMessage briefing with their recent emails, company info from your threads, and any open questions. No more scrambling for context.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Scheduled Send for Timezones">
    Compose emails anytime, then schedule delivery for the prospect's morning. Emails that land at 8:30 AM local time get opened more than emails sent at your convenience.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Dropped Ball Scanner">
    Daily scan for leads you haven't responded to. One morning summary tells you exactly which deals need attention today.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pro Tips

- **Track strategically:** Use `p o` on important outreach, not every email. When you see a prospect opened your proposal three times, that is your signal to call.
- **Snippet + personalize:** Start every cold email from a snippet, then add one personal line. You get consistency and speed without sounding like a template.
- **Batch prospect replies:** Use `j`/`k` to navigate, `Cmd+Enter` to draft, review, send. Process your entire prospect inbox in one focused block.
- **Ask the agent for summaries:** Before a QBR or pipeline review, ask *"List all prospects who haven't replied in the last 7 days"* for an instant stale-deal report.
