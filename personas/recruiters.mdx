Recruiting is a volume game with a personal touch. Slashy handles the volume so you can focus on the personal touch.

## Your Setup Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build Outreach Snippets">
    Press `n s` to create snippets for initial outreach, follow-ups, scheduling requests, and rejection emails. Insert with `Cmd+Shift+S` and personalize the opening line for each candidate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Email Tracking">
    Press `p o` on outreach emails to see when candidates open them. A candidate who opened three times but hasn't replied is worth a phone call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Booking Links">
    Set up scheduling pages in **Settings > Calendars > Booking Links** for phone screens, interviews, and coffee chats. Drop the link into any email with a snippet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Label Your Pipeline">
    Create importance labels for active candidates, hiring managers, and agencies. Hot candidates always surface first in your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Train AI on Your Tone">
    Send 5-10 replies using `Cmd+Enter` drafts. Edit each to match your warm-but-professional recruiting voice. The AI adapts within days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Automations For You

Open the AI agent sidebar (`Cmd+Shift+L`) and set up these automations:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Candidate Follow-Up">
    If a candidate hasn't replied in 3 days, Slashy drafts a follow-up and queues it for review. You approve or tweak -- no more manually tracking who needs a nudge.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Interview Prep Briefing">
    Before each interview, get a briefing with the candidate's email history, resume details from prior threads, and any scheduling notes. Walk in with full context.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Dropped Candidate Scanner">
    Daily morning scan for candidates you haven't responded to. One summary tells you exactly who is waiting on you today.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Hiring Manager Updates">
    Weekly summary of all candidate activity per role, sent to you via iMessage. Forward relevant updates to hiring managers without compiling them manually.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Workflow: Send 20 Personalized Outreach Emails in 15 Minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the snippet picker">
    Press `Cmd+Shift+S` in a new compose window to browse your outreach snippets. Pick the template that matches the role you are hiring for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insert the snippet and personalize">
    The snippet fills in the body, subject, and any AI variables. Edit the first sentence with something specific to the candidate -- their recent project, company, or LinkedIn headline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drop in your booking link">
    Include your scheduling page URL so candidates can self-book a phone screen. No back-and-forth needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable tracking and send">
    Toggle email tracking on in the compose window, then send. Repeat for each candidate -- the snippet does the heavy lifting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review opens the next morning">
    Press `p o` to check who opened. Candidates who opened 2-3 times without replying are warm -- reach out on LinkedIn or phone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Pro Tips

- **Personalize at scale:** Start every outreach from a snippet, then swap the first sentence for something specific to the candidate. You send 50 emails a day that each feel handwritten.
- **Track opens strategically:** When a candidate opens your email multiple times without replying, switch to a different channel -- call or LinkedIn message. They are interested but stuck.
- **Batch scheduling:** Include your booking link in every outreach snippet. Candidates self-schedule and you skip 10 back-and-forth emails per screen.
- **Pipeline review:** Ask the agent *"List all candidates who haven't replied in the last 5 days"* for an instant pipeline health check.
