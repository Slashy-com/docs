Executives get too much email and too little time. Slashy surfaces what matters, preps you for meetings, and drafts responses so you stay responsive without living in your inbox.

## Your Setup Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure VIP Labels">
    Go to **Settings > Labels** and create VIP labels for your board, CEO, direct reports, and key external contacts. Their emails jump to the top -- everything else gets triaged by importance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable iMessage Alerts">
    Connect your phone in **Settings > iMessage** and set alerts to VIP-only. You get texted when the board chair emails, not when a vendor follows up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Train AI on Your Voice">
    Reply to 5-10 emails using `Cmd+Enter` drafts and edit each one. The AI learns your executive communication style -- concise, decisive, and on-brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Calendar">
    Link Google Calendar in **Settings > Calendars**. Slashy uses it for pre-meeting briefings and inserting availability with `Shift+Cmd+A`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Executive Snippets">
    Press `n s` to create snippets for common responses: approvals, delegation requests, meeting follow-ups. Insert with `Cmd+Shift+S`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Automations For You

Open the AI agent sidebar (`Cmd+Shift+L`) and set up these automations:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="VIP Email Alerts">
    Instant iMessage notification when board members, your CEO, or flagged contacts send you an email. Never miss a critical message even when you are in back-to-back meetings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Weekly Email Summary">
    Every Monday morning, receive a digest of the past week's email activity: key threads, unanswered messages, and trends. Review your inbox landscape in 2 minutes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Meeting Prep Briefing">
    15 minutes before each meeting, get a briefing with attendee email history, open threads, and relevant context. Walk into every meeting informed without manual prep.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Delegate with Context">
    Tell the agent *"Forward this to [ea@company.com] with a summary and next steps."* Your EA or chief of staff gets the full picture and clear instructions in one forwarded email.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pro Tips

- **Triage in 5 minutes:** VIP labels + importance sorting means your top 10 emails are the only ones that matter. Use `j`/`k` to navigate, `Cmd+Enter` to draft, `e` to archive the rest.
- **AI drafts for speed:** Accept the AI draft for routine responses. Edit only when nuance matters. Most executives cut reply time by 60%.
- **Board prep on demand:** Ask the agent *"Summarize all board-related emails from the last month"* before any board meeting for a complete communication recap.
- **Delegate, don't forward:** Instead of forwarding raw threads to your EA, ask the agent to add context and action items. Your EA spends less time decoding and more time executing.

## Sample Agent Commands

Try these in the agent sidebar (`Cmd+Shift+L`):

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Triage & Summaries">
    - *"What are my most important unread emails right now?"*
    - *"Summarize all board-related emails from the last month"*
    - *"Give me a weekly recap of key threads and who I owe replies to"*
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Delegation">
    - *"Forward this to [ea@company.com] with a summary and next steps"*
    - *"Draft a response delegating this to [direct report] with context"*
    - *"Send [EA] my schedule conflicts for next week"*
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Decisions">
    - *"Draft an approval email for this request -- brief and decisive"*
    - *"Summarize this thread and highlight the key decision needed"*
    - *"What commitments have I made in email this week?"*
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
