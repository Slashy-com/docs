Slashy has built-in booking pages -- no separate Calendly or Cal.com subscription needed. Create a shareable link, configure availability, and let people book directly. It checks your calendar in real time.

## Create Your First Booking Link

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to **Settings > Calendars** (`Cmd+,`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Find the **Booking Links** section and click **Create Booking Link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Set duration, buffer time, booking window, URL slug, and optional questions (see below).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Share">
    Your link is live at `slashy.com/book/yourname`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configuration Options

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Duration">
    15 min (quick intro), 30 min (standard), or 60 min (deep dive). Create multiple links for different durations.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Buffer Time">
    Block time before and after meetings to prevent back-to-back scheduling.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Booking Window">
    **Minimum notice:** e.g., 4 hours (prevents last-minute bookings). **Maximum advance:** e.g., 2 weeks out.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Custom URL">
    `slashy.com/book/yourname/quick-chat` or `slashy.com/book/yourname/strategy-session`.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Booking Questions">
    Guests answer before booking. Answers appear in the calendar event description.
    - "What would you like to discuss?"
    - "What is your company and role?"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Sharing Your Link

- **In emails:** Paste directly or use Insert Availability (`Shift+Cmd+A`) for inline time slots
- **In your signature:** Add to your Slashy email signature
- **Via the agent:** *"Draft a reply suggesting a meeting and include my booking link"*

## How Guests Book

Guests see a date picker, available time slots for the selected day, and a form for their details. Once booked, a calendar event is created for both parties with a conferencing link auto-included (if Zoom/Meet is connected).

Confirmation emails include cancel and reschedule links.

## Tips

- **Create multiple links** for different purposes (15-min quick chat, 30-min strategy session)
- **Set 10-15 min buffers** to prevent meeting fatigue
- **Keep questions to 1-2** -- too many fields and people abandon the form
- **Connect conferencing** in Settings > Calendars so meeting links are auto-generated
