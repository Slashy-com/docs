Automations turn your inbox from a to-do list into a system that handles itself. Each one takes under two minutes to set up through the AI agent sidebar (`p a`).

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="1. Meeting Confirmation Follow-Ups">
    Auto-checks your calendar when someone confirms a meeting and drafts a confirmation or flags conflicts.

    Open the agent sidebar and say: *"When someone confirms a meeting time, check my calendar for conflicts. If none, draft a short confirmation. If there's a conflict, message me on iMessage."*

    <Frame><img src="/images/screenshots/automation-meeting-confirmation.png" alt="Agent sidebar showing completed meeting confirmation automation" /></Frame>

    **Tip:** Add *"only for meetings with external participants"* to skip internal confirmations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2. Receipt Forwarding (Expensify/Ramp)">
    Forwards receipt and payment confirmation emails to your expense tool automatically.

    Tell the agent: *"When I receive a receipt or payment confirmation, forward it to receipts@expensify.com"*

    Replace the address with your Expensify, Ramp, or Brex intake email.

    **Tip:** Be specific — say *"receipts and invoices, but not subscription renewal notices"* to keep your expense tool clean.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="3. Daily Dropped Ball Scanner">
    Every morning, scans for emails you sent with no reply, emails you never responded to, and commitments you haven't fulfilled. Sends a summary via iMessage.

    Tell the agent: *"Every weekday at 8am, scan my inbox for dropped balls — emails I sent with no reply in 7 days, emails I didn't respond to, and commitments I haven't followed up on. Send summary on iMessage."*

    The agent creates a recurring reminder via AWS EventBridge.

    **Tip:** Adjust the time to when you start your day. Say *"email me the summary"* if you prefer email over iMessage.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="4. OTP/2FA Forwarding to iMessage">
    Instantly forwards verification codes from email to iMessage so you don't context-switch.

    Requires the [iMessage agent](/how-to-guides/imessage-setup) to be connected.

    Tell the agent: *"When I receive an email with a verification code or OTP, send me the code on iMessage immediately."*

    The agent extracts just the code number, not the full email body.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="5. VIP Email Alerts">
    Alerts you on iMessage with a visual preview when specific people email you.

    Tell the agent: *"When I receive an email from jane@bigclient.com or anyone @investor-fund.com, message me on iMessage with a preview."*

    **Tip:** You can also trigger on conditions like *"any email with 'urgent' in the subject."*
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="6. Meeting Prep Briefing">
    Before each meeting, researches attendees via LinkedIn and your email history, then sends a structured briefing on iMessage.

    Tell the agent: *"10 minutes before any meeting with external attendees, prep me for the meeting and send the briefing on iMessage."*

    The briefing includes attendee profiles, recent email threads, key topics, and suggested questions.

    **Tip:** Narrow with *"only for meetings with people outside my company"* or *"only meetings with 'investor' in the title."*
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="7. Post-Meeting Follow-Up">
    Drafts a follow-up email to all attendees with action items after a meeting ends. Integrates with Granola notes if connected.

    Tell the agent: *"5 minutes after any external meeting ends, draft a follow-up email to all attendees with a summary and next steps. Don't send — just draft."*

    **Tip:** Connect Granola in **Settings > Integrations** for more accurate follow-ups.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="8. Weekly Email Summary">
    Every Friday (or your preferred time), reviews your entire week of email and sends a categorized summary: resolved, pending, owed replies, and threads to watch.

    Tell the agent: *"Every Friday at 3pm, review my email from this week. Summarize what's resolved, pending, who I owe replies to, and important threads. Send via iMessage."*

    **Tip:** Change to Monday mornings for a "week ahead" view.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="9. Customer Follow-Up Checker">
    Monitors key customer threads and flags any where a customer is waiting on you for more than N days.

    Tell the agent: *"Every weekday at 9am, check if any customers from @bigclient.com have emails waiting for my reply for more than 2 days. Message me on iMessage."*

    **Tip:** Instead of listing domains, say *"any email from someone I've exchanged more than 10 emails with"* to use relationship data.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="10. Email Open Alerts">
    Alerts you when a recipient opens a tracked email. Filters out auto-opens from Gmail Image Proxy, Apple Mail Privacy Protection, and Microsoft SafeLinks.

    Verify tracking is enabled in **Settings > Account** under "Email Tracking," then tell the agent: *"When a tracked email I sent to @investor-fund.com is opened for the first time, message me on iMessage."*

    **Tip:** Press `p o` anytime to view the Recent Opens panel. See [Email Tracking](/how-to-guides/email-tracking) for details on tracking limitations.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Managing Automations

All automations live in **Settings > Automations** (`Cmd+,`). You can toggle, delete, or check recent activity. You can also manage via the agent sidebar — ask it to *"list my automations"* or *"disable the receipt forwarding trigger."*
