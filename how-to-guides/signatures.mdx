## Removing "Sent with Slashy"

### Globally

1. Go to **Settings** (`Cmd+,`) > **Account**
2. Toggle off **"Sent with Slashy" Signature**

All future emails will be sent without the footer. Already-sent emails are unaffected.

<Frame><img src="/images/screenshots/settings-sent-with-slashy-toggle.png" alt="Settings showing the Sent with Slashy toggle in off position" /></Frame>

### Per Email

Delete the Slashy signature directly from the compose area before sending. Only affects that one email.

## Your Email Signature

Slashy detects your signature from Gmail during setup. The AI uses it when generating drafts.

To update: **Settings** (`Cmd+,`) > **Account** > **Email Signature** field.

<Frame><img src="/images/screenshots/settings-email-signature.png" alt="Settings showing the Email Signature field" /></Frame>

<Note>This is separate from your Gmail signature settings. It controls what the AI references when drafting.</Note>

## Signatures in Replies

By default, Slashy does not include your full signature in replies — most people don't repeat it in every thread message.

To change: **Settings > Account** > toggle **"Include Signature in Replies"** on.

## How Signatures Work with AI Drafts

The AI knows whether to use your full signature block or just your first name, your preferred sign-off, and whether the context is formal or informal.

If the AI uses the wrong sign-off, update your signature in Settings or tell the agent: *"Remember: I sign off with just my first name, no closing phrase."*

## Per-Recipient Signatures

Not yet a built-in setting, but achievable with agent memories:

Open the sidebar (`p a`) and say: *"Remember: when emailing anyone @bigclient.com, use my full formal signature. When emailing my team, just sign with my first name."*

This works for AI-generated drafts. For manual emails, use a snippet (`Cmd+Shift+S`) with your formal signature saved as a template.

## Multiple Inboxes

Each connected Gmail account has its own detected signature. Switch the sending account using the **From** dropdown in the compose window.
