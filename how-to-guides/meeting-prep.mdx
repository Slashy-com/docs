---
title: "Auto-Prep for Every Meeting"
description: "Set up automatic meeting briefings with attendee profiles, email history, and suggested talking points."
---

Slashy's AI agent researches every attendee before your meeting -- pulling LinkedIn profiles, email history, and recent news -- then delivers a structured prep doc. Set it up once, and it runs before every qualifying meeting.

## What the Prep Doc Includes

- **Attendee profiles:** Name, title, company, LinkedIn URL, short bio
- **Contact enrichment:** Company size, funding stage, industry
- **Email history:** Recent threads, open action items, last contact date
- **Web search:** Recent news about attendees or their company
- **AI memories:** Stored facts from past interactions
- **Suggested talking points:** Based on the research

## One-Time Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the agent sidebar">
    Press `Cmd+Shift+L`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the automation">
    Type: *"Create a calendar automation: 30 minutes before any meeting with external attendees, research each attendee and send me a meeting prep briefing on iMessage."*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    The agent creates a calendar trigger. Every external meeting gets a prep doc on your phone 30 minutes before.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customize the Trigger

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Timing">
    - *"10 minutes before"* (last-minute prep)
    - *"1 hour before"* (review over coffee)
    - *"The evening before"* (next-day prep)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Which Meetings">
    - *"Only for meetings with people I haven't met before"*
    - *"Only for meetings with 'investor' or 'board' in the title"*
    - *"For all meetings, including internal"*
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Delivery">
    - iMessage (requires iMessage agent)
    - Email to your inbox
    - Note in the agent sidebar
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Improving Quality Over Time

The prep docs get better as you use Slashy:

- **Memories build up** from email interactions -- contact facts appear in future preps
- **Email history deepens** the longer you use Slashy as primary
- **Enrichment data refreshes** each time, staying current

<Tip>Manage the automation in **Settings > Automations**. Disable it temporarily for vacations or adjust the trigger criteria anytime.</Tip>
