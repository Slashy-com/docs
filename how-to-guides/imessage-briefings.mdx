---
title: "Daily Briefings via iMessage"
description: "Set up recurring morning briefings with meetings, VIP emails, and pending follow-ups delivered to your phone."
---

Set up a recurring daily briefing and every morning get a text summarizing your day: meetings, unread VIP emails, and pending follow-ups. Read it with coffee, before opening your laptop.

<Info>The iMessage agent must be connected first. See [Setting Up the iMessage Agent](/how-to-guides/imessage-setup) if you haven't done this yet.</Info>

## Set Up Your Morning Briefing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the agent sidebar">
    Press `Cmd+Shift+L`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the automation">
    Type: *"Set up a recurring agent reminder: every weekday at 8am, send me a morning briefing on iMessage. Include today's calendar meetings, unread emails from VIPs, and any pending follow-ups."*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    The agent creates a scheduled reminder. Your first briefing arrives the next weekday at 8am.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the Briefing Includes

A typical briefing:

> **Good morning. Here is your briefing for Tuesday, March 24.**
>
> **Calendar (4 meetings):**
> 9:00am - Team standup (Google Meet)
> 11:00am - Sarah Chen, product review (Zoom)
> 2:00pm - Investor call with Mark at Sequoia (Zoom)
>
> **Unread from VIPs (2):**
> Mark from Sequoia - RE: Q1 metrics (received 11pm)
> Lisa from BigCorp - Contract renewal question (received 7am)
>
> **Pending follow-ups (1):**
> You emailed David about the partnership 3 days ago. No reply yet.

## Customize Content

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Add Sections">
    - *"Include a count of total unread emails per label"*
    - *"Include tasks due today or overdue"*
    - *"Include emails where someone is waiting on me for 2+ days"*
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Remove Sections">
    - *"Don't include follow-ups, just calendar and VIP emails"*
    - *"Skip recurring internal meetings"*
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Change VIPs">
    - *"VIPs are anyone from @sequoia.com, my co-founder alex@mycompany.com, and anyone in my Investors label"*
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Adjust Schedule

- *"Change my briefing to 7:30am"*
- *"Send every day including weekends"*
- *"Also send an end-of-day briefing at 5pm with just pending follow-ups"*
- *"Pause the briefing for the next week while I'm on vacation"*

## Respond and Act

Text back to act on items immediately:

- *"Draft a reply to Mark's email confirming we'll have metrics by Friday"*
- *"Cancel my 4:30 design review and let Alex know I need to reschedule"*
- *"Follow up with David about the partnership -- say we'd love to move forward"*

Actions queue up so they're waiting when you open Slashy on your laptop.
