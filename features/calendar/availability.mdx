Stop the back-and-forth scheduling. Press `Shift+Cmd+A` while composing an email, select open slots on your calendar, and Slashy inserts a clean, formatted list of available times into your message.

## Inserting Availability

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open composer">
    Start a new email, reply, or forward.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate availability mode">
    Press `Shift+Cmd+A`. Your calendar opens in selection mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select time slots">
    Click on times that work for you. Selected slots are highlighted. Click again to deselect. Your existing events are visible so you avoid conflicts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm your selections. Slashy inserts formatted text at your cursor position.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The recipient sees a clean list like:

> Tuesday, March 25 — 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM
> Wednesday, March 26 — 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
> Thursday, March 27 — 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM

## Meet With: See Others' Busy Times

When scheduling with specific people, overlay their calendar on yours to find mutual openings.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the calendar">
    Switch to calendar view with `2` or `p c`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate Meet With">
    Enter the email addresses of people you want to meet with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the overlays">
    Each person gets their own color. Busy times appear as colored blocks on your calendar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find open slots">
    Gaps with no overlays are times when everyone is free.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/screenshots/sharing-your-availability-2.png" alt="Calendar with color-coded busy-time overlays from multiple attendees" />
</Frame>

<Info>Slashy uses the Google Calendar FreeBusy API. You see when others are busy but not their event details.</Info>

## Combining with Booking Links

For the smoothest workflow:

1. Create a booking link in **Settings > Calendars** with your general availability windows.
2. Press `Shift+Cmd+A` to insert specific times into your email.
3. Include your booking link URL so the recipient can confirm in one click.

This gives recipients two options: pick from specific times or visit your booking page to find another slot.

## Quick Reference

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| Insert availability | `Shift+Cmd+A` in composer |
| Switch to calendar | `2` or `p c` |
| Navigate calendar | Arrow keys, `t` for today |
| Create booking link | **Settings > Calendars > New Booking Link** |
