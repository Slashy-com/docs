Slashy supports multiple Gmail accounts in one interface. Connect work and personal inboxes without signing in and out.

## Connect Another Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to **Settings > Account** (`Cmd+,`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Account">
    Click **Connect Account** under Connected Accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Sign in with Google and grant the same permissions as your first account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync">
    Initial sync takes 2-15 minutes depending on inbox size. Labels are set up automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switching Accounts

Use `Alt+1`, `Alt+2`, `Alt+3` to switch accounts. `Alt+0` opens **All mode** -- a unified inbox showing emails from every account interleaved by date.

## All Mode

In All mode:

- Importance labels combine emails from all accounts under each tab
- Search covers all accounts at once
- When composing, choose which account to send from via the **From** dropdown
- Replies automatically use the account that received the email

## What Merges vs. Stays Separate

| Merged Across Accounts | Separate Per Account |
|---|---|
| Inbox view (All mode) | Importance labels |
| Search | Gmail labels |
| AI Memories | Calendar |
| AI Agent | Signatures |
| Automations | Contacts |

## Per-Inbox Settings

Each account has independent importance labels, signatures, notification preferences, and undo send delay. Switch to the target account first, then open **Settings**.

## Tips

<Tip>Use All mode as your default for unified inbox processing. Switch to a single account with `Alt+1`/`Alt+2` when you need to focus.</Tip>

- **Set different importance labels per account.** Work and personal inboxes have different classification needs.
- **The AI agent has context from all accounts.** Ask: "Did anyone from Sequoia email either of my accounts this week?"
