Slashy includes a full calendar built into your email client. Your Google Calendar syncs automatically when you connect Gmail -- no separate setup.

## Google Calendar Sync

Two-way, continuous sync. Changes in Slashy sync to Google Calendar and vice versa. Multiple Gmail accounts sync independently.

## Choose Which Calendars to Show

1. Open **Settings** (`Cmd+,`) > **Calendars**.
2. Toggle each calendar on or off. Only enabled calendars appear in the calendar view.
3. Assign colors to distinguish work from personal events.

Changes take effect immediately.

## Timezone Display

Slashy uses your system timezone. Incoming invites auto-convert to your local time. When creating events with remote attendees, times display in your timezone -- attendees see their own.

## RSVP from Email

Calendar invites show **Accept**, **Maybe**, and **Decline** buttons directly in the email thread. No need to open Google Calendar.

<Tip>Press `Tab` on a calendar invite email to accept and archive in one action.</Tip>

## Creating Events

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Click and Drag">
    In calendar view, click a time slot and drag to set duration. The event editor opens with time pre-filled.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Keyboard">
    Press `c` in calendar view or click the create button. Set date and time in the editor.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Event Editor Fields

| Field | Details |
|---|---|
| **Title** | Event name |
| **Time** | Start/end. Drag on calendar to reschedule later. |
| **Location** | Address or room name |
| **Attendees** | Add by email with autocomplete |
| **Conferencing** | Google Meet, Zoom, Teams, Webex, or custom link |
| **Recurrence** | Daily, weekly, monthly, or custom pattern |

Delete with `Backspace`. Undo with `z`.

## Share Your Availability

Instead of listing free times manually:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open availability picker">
    Press `Shift+Cmd+A` in the compose window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select time slots">
    Pick available times on the calendar overlay. Conflicts are visible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insert">
    Slashy inserts formatted availability into your email. Recipients see times in their timezone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Booking Links

Slashy's built-in alternative to Calendly. Create shareable pages where anyone can book time on your calendar.

1. **Settings** (`Cmd+,`) > **Calendars** > **Booking Links** > **Create Booking Link**.
2. Configure **duration** (15/30/45/60 min), **buffer** between meetings, and **booking window** (how far ahead).
3. Share the public URL.

Booking links check your calendar in real-time -- no double-bookings.

## Meet With (Busy Time Overlay)

Find times that work for multiple people:

1. Open calendar view (`2`).
2. Use **Meet With** and add contacts.
3. Their busy times appear as shaded blocks alongside your events.
4. Click a gap to create an event.

Works for anyone in your org or who has shared their calendar with you.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

| Shortcut | Action |
|---|---|
| `2` | Switch to calendar |
| `1` | Switch to email |
| `t` | Jump to today |
| `c` | Create event |
| `Backspace` | Delete event |
| `Shift+Cmd+A` | Insert availability (in compose) |
