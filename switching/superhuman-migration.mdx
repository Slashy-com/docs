You chose Superhuman because you wanted fast, keyboard-first email. Slashy shares that DNA -- plus an AI agent that actually does things, not just summarizes.

## What Feels Familiar

These work the same way you are used to:

- **Keyboard-first design** -- navigate, triage, and reply without touching the mouse
- **AI drafts** -- select a thread and get an instant reply draft
- **Split inbox** -- importance labels sort email automatically (like Superhuman's splits)
- **Snippets** -- templated text you insert with a shortcut
- **Read receipts** -- see when your email was opened
- **Scheduled send** -- compose now, send later

## What's Different (and Better)

| Superhuman | Slashy |
|---|---|
| AI writes drafts you review | AI agent drafts, sends, schedules, and automates on your behalf |
| No automations | Background automations: dropped ball scanner, meeting prep, receipt forwarding |
| No iMessage integration | iMessage alerts, summaries, and OTP forwarding |
| Calendar in sidebar only | Full calendar view with booking links, availability insert, Meet With overlay |
| Calendly needed for scheduling | Built-in booking links -- no third-party tool |
| Per-seat pricing adds up | No per-seat pricing anxiety |

## Feature Mapping

**Superhuman Social Insights** become **Slashy Contact Memories**. Instead of pulling LinkedIn data, Slashy builds context from your email history -- what you have discussed, meeting notes, and facts you add manually. Open the contact sidebar with `p i`.

**Superhuman Snippets** become **Slashy Snippets**. Create with `n s`, insert with `Cmd+Shift+S`. Slashy snippets are more powerful -- they support AI-powered variable substitution (e.g., `{{first_name}}` auto-fills from contact data).

**Superhuman Splits** become **Slashy Importance Labels**. Same concept (auto-sorted inbox sections), but powered by AI classification that learns from your corrections.

## Setting Up Snippets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a snippet">
    Press `n s` to open the snippet creator. Give it a name, shortcut, and body text.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insert in compose">
    While composing, press `Cmd+Shift+S` to search and insert a snippet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use variables">
    Add `{{first_name}}`, `{{company}}`, or custom fields. Slashy fills them from contact data automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Migration Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your Gmail account">
    Sign up at [slashy.com](https://slashy.com) and connect via Google OAuth. All email, labels, and contacts sync automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Recreate your Superhuman Splits as labels">
    Go to **Settings > Labels** and set up importance labels to match your Superhuman Splits. The AI classification learns from corrections.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Migrate snippets">
    Press `n s` to create each snippet. Copy your Superhuman snippet text and add AI variables like `{{first_name}}` for extra power.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up automations">
    Open the agent sidebar (`Cmd+Shift+L`) and set up your first automation -- start with the Dropped Ball Scanner. This is functionality Superhuman does not offer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add AI memories">
    **Settings > Memories** -- describe your communication style. This replaces Superhuman's limited tone settings with full-context personalization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel Superhuman">
    Once you have used Slashy for a week and confirmed your workflow is set up, cancel your Superhuman subscription.
  </Step>
</Steps>
