---

## March 2026

### v2.14 -- Top-Section Grouping (March 23)

Pin unread or starred emails to the top of any label view. **Settings > Account > Top Section** -- choose None, Unread at Top, or Starred at Top.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/screenshots/what-s-new-in-slashy.png" alt="Top-section grouping feature showing pinned unread emails" />
</Frame>

### v2.13 -- Calendar RSVP on Mobile (March 16)

Accept, decline, or tentatively accept calendar invitations directly from the Slashy mobile app. Works for Google Calendar and Outlook/Teams invitations.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/screenshots/mobile-rsvp-buttons.png" alt="Mobile app showing RSVP buttons on a calendar invite email" />
</Frame>

### v2.12 -- Desktop Auto-Update (March 9)

The desktop app now updates silently in the background. A small prompt appears at bottom-left when a new version is ready -- click to restart and apply.

### v2.11 -- Agent Sidebar Improvements (March 2)

The AI Agent sidebar (`Cmd+Shift+L`) now supports:
- View and resume past conversations
- Rename conversations
- Upload files (PDFs, images, docs) for context
- Real-time tool-use indicators

---

## February 2026

### v2.10 -- AI Autocomplete in Compose (February 20)

Ghost text suggestions appear as you type. Press `Tab` to accept. Works in compose, reply, and forward modes. Predictions are based on thread context, your writing style, and memories.

### v2.9 -- Booking Links (February 12)

Built-in scheduling pages -- no Calendly needed. **Settings > Calendars** to create links with configurable duration, availability, and buffer time. Guests can cancel or reschedule without an account.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/screenshots/booking-link-config.png" alt="Booking link configuration with duration, availability windows, and public URL" />
</Frame>

### v2.8 -- Bulk Unsubscribe Improvements (February 5)

Cleaner sender list with one-click unsubscribe. Bulk select multiple senders. Unsubscribed senders are auto-blocked so future emails are archived.

---

## January 2026

### v2.7 -- AI Label Feedback Loop (January 25)

Moving an email to a different importance label now trains the AI. Classification accuracy improves over time for your specific patterns.

### v2.6 -- Insert Availability (January 15)

Press `Shift+Cmd+A` while composing to insert available time slots with a scheduling link. Recipients click to book.

### v2.5 -- Meet With: Overlay Busy Times (January 8)

In calendar view, overlay other people's busy times to find open slots without back-and-forth.

## Submit Feature Requests

- **Direct message** the Slashy team via iMessage, WhatsApp, Telegram, or Slack
- **In-app support** -- describe the feature and why your current workflow falls short
- **Tell the AI Agent** (`Cmd+Shift+L`) -- the team reviews agent conversations for common requests

<Tip>The best feature requests include: what you are trying to do, how you currently work around it, and how often you hit the problem.</Tip>
